Projects

Explore our cutting-edge research projects leveraging state-of-the-art cyber-physical systems and advanced robotics to solve real-world industrial challenges.

Demo Videos

Intelligent Inspection System for Unmanned Vehicles: AI as the Brain

An autonomous inspection system leveraging deep learning and computer vision to enable unmanned vehicles to detect defects, anomalies, and safety hazards in industrial environments with human-level accuracy.

Privacy-Aware Fall Alert

A privacy-preserving fall detection system using edge computing and federated learning to monitor elderly individuals while ensuring data security and personal privacy protection without compromising detection accuracy.

Uniting Human-Robot Intelligence for Complex Tasks in Unreliable Environments

A hybrid human-robot collaboration framework that combines human cognitive abilities with robotic precision to tackle complex manipulation tasks in uncertain, dynamic environments through adaptive learning and real-time coordination.

Be the Robot: VR Head Tracking for Robot Vision

An immersive teleoperation system that translates natural human head movements into robot camera control through VR head tracking, enabling intuitive remote operation and enhanced situational awareness for complex robotic tasks.
